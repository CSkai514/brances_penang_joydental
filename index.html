Frequently Asked Questions

Is braces painful?

Mild discomfort at the start and after tightening, but it's very manageable and fades quickly.

Can adults do braces?

Yes, absolutely! It is very common. We treat many adult professionals using ceramic or clear aligners.

How often do I need to visit?

Usually every 4-6 weeks for adjustments, depending on the type of braces you choose.
